The DVSA follows the GOV.UK Design System contribution criteria. The only difference is that contributions will be reviewed by the DVSA’s Design System Working Group.

The contents of the Design System must be of a high quality and meet user needs. To guarantee this, all components and patterns need to meet certain criteria.

DVSA design system contribution criteria

As a working group, we have put together a quality gate that is used to decide if a component is ready to be published in the DVSA design system, these are organised into the following checklists:

  • fundamental – a component or pattern must meet this criteria before being published in the DVSA design system
  • recommended – we would prefer to proceed with these but they are not vital to a component or pattern being published
