Use the numberplate display component to help users identify a vehicle.

When to use this component

Use this component when it is important to display the numberplate of a vehicle to the user.

When not to use this component

Do not use this component to show anything other than a numberplate of a vehicle.

Do not modify the styling of this component. It is consistent with the style of a UK numberplate.

How it works

The component displays letters and numbers in the style of a UK numberplate.

This is to aid the user in understanding the numberplate of the vehicle they are looking at alongside relevant information.
